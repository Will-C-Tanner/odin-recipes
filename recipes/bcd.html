Buffalo Chicken Dip Recipe

Description

A creamy, yet tangy dip, that tastes just like Buffalo chicken wings! Best served pipin' hot with celery sticks and crackers. Mix in some bleu cheese on the side for added punch.

Total cook time will be about 45 minutes yielding 20 servings, appropriate for 4-5 people.

Ingredients

  • 2 (10 ounce) cans chunk chicken, drained
  • 2 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup Ranch dressing
  • 3/4 cup pepper sauce (i.e. Frank's Red Hot®
  • 1 1/2 cups shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 1 bunch celery, cleaned and cut into 4 inch pieces
  • 1 (8 ounce) box chicken-flavored crackers

Steps

  1. Heat chicken and hot sauce in a skillet over medium heat, until heated throughout.
  2. Stir in cream cheese and ranch dressing.Cook, stirring until blended and warm.
  3. Mix in half of the shredded cheese, and transfer mixture into a slow cooker.
  4. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the top, cover the top, and cook on "Low" setting until hot and bubbly.
  5. Serve with celery sticks and crackers. Bon appétit!
